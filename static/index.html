Use Shinobi
01

Finds the moments

Claude reads your full transcript — not just keywords — and scores every segment for hook strength, emotional payoff, and how well it stands alone. It picks the moments that actually make people stop scrolling.

02

Cuts the silence

Dead air, "ums," and long pauses are detected automatically and trimmed out — frame-accurate — so every clip feels tight and high-energy without you touching a timeline.

03

Goes vertical

Each clip is reframed to 9:16 with smart cropping that tracks faces and motion, so your subject stays centered — no black bars, no stretching.

04

Burns in captions

Animated, word-by-word subtitles are burned directly into the video — styled to match the energy of short-form platforms and ready to post.

05

Writes the copy

For every clip, Shinobi writes a title, description, tags, and a Reddit-ready framing — tuned to your tone, audience, and niche.

06

Posts everywhere

Connect your accounts once, then publish to YouTube Shorts, X, and Reddit simultaneously with a single click.
